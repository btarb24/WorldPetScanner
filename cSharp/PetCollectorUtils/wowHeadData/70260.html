Tiny White Carp

This NPC can be found in Kun-Lai Summit .
Profession: Mists of Pandaria Fishing

These hardy fish can survive in a wide range of climates, from the snow capped mountains of Kun-Lai Summit to the temperate, salty waters of the oceans around Pandaria. Dedicated fishermen can sometimes befriend one.
