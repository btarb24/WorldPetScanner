Strand Crab

This NPC can be found in The Cape of Stranglethorn  (150), Northern Stranglethorn  (95), Spires of Arak  (14), and Swamp of Sorrows  (8).
Pet Battle: Darkshore, Dragonblight, Dun Morogh, Gilneas, Kezan, Northern Stranglethorn, Spires of Arak, Swamp of Sorrows, The Cape of Stranglethorn, The Lost Isles

The low tide reveals the colonies of strand crabs and the sound of their clicking dance as they feed upon small creatures hidden in the sand.
