Beetle

This NPC can be found in Northern Stranglethorn  (93), The Cape of Stranglethorn  (63), Un'Goro Crater  (63), Silithus  (50), Badlands  (12), Felwood  (4), The Azure Span  (2), The Waking Shores  (2), Aberrus, the Shadowed Crucible , and Amirdrassil, the Dream's Hope .
Pet Battle: Badlands, Eastern Plaguelands, Felwood, Northern Stranglethorn, Silithus, The Cape of Stranglethorn, Un'Goro Crater, The Waking Shores, The Azure Span, Thaldraszus

A hardy insect that can dwell in just about any climate, the beetle is known to thrive on battlefields after the fighting has ended.
