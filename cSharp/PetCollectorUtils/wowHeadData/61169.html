Roach

This NPC can be found in Drustvar  (16), Undercity  (16), Duskwood  (11), Azshara  (9), Desolace  (9), Ashenvale  (7), The Cape of Stranglethorn  (7), Redridge Mountains  (6), and Thousand Needles  (6).
Pet Battle: Ashenvale, Azshara, Desolace, Dun Morogh, Duskwood, Gilneas, Gilneas City, Howling Fjord, Icecrown Citadel, Kezan, Loch Modan, Northern Stranglethorn, Razorfen Downs, Redridge Mountains, Stonetalon Mountains, The Cape of Stranglethorn, The Culling of Stratholme, Thousand Needles, Tirisfal Glades

A nuisance to most, roaches' incredible survivability sets them apart from other species.
