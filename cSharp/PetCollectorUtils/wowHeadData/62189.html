Alpine Chipmunk

This NPC can be found in Winterspring  (191), Stonetalon Mountains  (71), Mount Hyjal  (66), Highmountain  (22), Drustvar  (6), The Azure Span  (2), and Tiragarde Sound  (2).
Pet Battle: Highmountain, Mount Hyjal, Stonetalon Mountains, Winterspring, The Azure Span

Many an explorer has been entranced by the adorable nature of the alpine chipmunk, failing to notice its small teeth on frostbitten digits.
