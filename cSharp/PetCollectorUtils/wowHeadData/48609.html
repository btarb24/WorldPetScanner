Clockwork Gnome

This NPC can be found in Arathi Highlands , Badlands , Burning Steppes , Searing Gorge , The Hinterlands , Twilight Highlands , and Wetlands .
Profession: Archaeology

Thought to be an original construct of the titans, the clockwork gnome has some gears smaller than the eye can see.
